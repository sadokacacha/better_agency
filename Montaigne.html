Hôtel Montaigne

Hotel Montaigne is located in the centre of Cannes, just a 5-minute walk from La Croisette. Guests have paid access to the 250 m² Spa, which offers indoor swimming pool, hot tub and beauty treatments. The hotel is non-smoking throughout.
